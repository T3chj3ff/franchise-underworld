The Table feeds the city.

Lumenridge is a late-90s city built on shifts, silence, and a fragile truce between the factions that feed it. When a pastor known for keeping the peace is found dead, the city calls it an accident. Julian Pike knows better.


A former delivery driver turned blacklisted reporter, Pike uncovers fragments of a buried archive known as the Burning Crown, proof that the city's worst disaster was staged decades ago.


As factions tighten control and routes begin to disappear, Pike is forced back into a world that profits from forgetting. In Lumenridge, truth is currency, and silence keeps the city alive.
